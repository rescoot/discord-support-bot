👋 **Welcome to the UNU Community!**

Glad you're here. A few pointers to get started:

**Likely relevant channels:**
• `#allgemein` – general chat (mostly German)
• `#roller-support` – scooter hardware/firmware issues
• `#app-support` – unu app, Bluetooth, waking
• `#ressourcen` – spare parts, 3D prints, docs

**The bot can help:**
• `/faq` – common questions on battery, app, firmware, repairs…
• `/glossary` – technical terms explained (MDB, DBC, CBB, AUX…)
• `/diagnose` – guided troubleshooters (AUX battery, app connection)

**Tip for asking in `#roller-support`:**
You'll get help fastest if you say three things:
1. **What did you do?** (held the keycard, connected the app, inserted the battery…)
2. **What did you expect to happen?**
3. **What happened instead?**

If relevant:
• **Year** — it's in the VIN; decode with <https://rescoot.org/vin-decoder>
• **Firmware versions** — with the scooter **powered on**, pull **both brake levers together for at least 5 seconds**; the "Versionsbox" shows MDB, DBC, nRF versions plus CBB state of charge. Target versions in `/faq firmware`. *(If the scooter won't power on at all, version doesn't matter yet — describe the won't-power-on symptom instead.)*

Most of the community is German-speaking but English is fine too. Have fun! 🛵
