👋 **Welcome to the UNU Community!**

Glad you're here. A few pointers to get started:

**Likely relevant channels:**
• `#allgemein` – general chat (mostly German)
• `#support` – scooter and app issues
• `#ressourcen` – spare parts, 3D prints, docs

**The bot can help:**
• `/faq` – common questions on battery, app, firmware, repairs…
• `/glossary` – technical terms explained (MDB, DBC, AUX…)
• `/diagnose` – guided troubleshooters (AUX battery, app connection, display)

**Tip for asking in `#support`:**
You'll get help fastest if you say three things:
1. **What did you do?** (held the keycard, connected the app, inserted the battery…)
2. **What did you expect to happen?**
3. **What happened instead?**

If relevant:
• **Year** — in the VIN; decode with <https://sunshine.rescoot.org/vin-decoder>
• **Firmware versions** — power on the scooter, hold both brake levers together for at least 5 s; the Versionsbox appears with MDB, DBC, nRF and CBB charge level. Targets: `/faq firmware`. If the scooter won't power on at all, version doesn't matter yet.

Most of the community is German-speaking but English is fine too. Have fun! 🛵
